## Quiz

1. What is CS research?
   - **A)** Only programming
   - **B)** Advancing knowledge and solving open problems in computer science
   - **C)** Only applications
   - **D)** Only theory

2. What is P vs NP?
   - **A)** Solved problem
   - **B)** Open problem asking if P equals NP (Millennium Prize)
   - **C)** Easy problem
   - **D)** Simple problem

3. What are current AI research trends?
   - **A)** Only old methods
   - **B)** Large language models, multimodal AI, foundation models, AI safety
   - **C)** No trends
   - **D)** Only theory

**Answers:**
1. **B** - CS research advances knowledge through theoretical, applied, and experimental work
2. **B** - P vs NP asks whether problems solvable in polynomial time equal those verifiable in polynomial time; unsolved Millennium Prize Problem
3. **B** - Current trends include large language models (GPT), multimodal AI, foundation models, and AI safety/alignment research
