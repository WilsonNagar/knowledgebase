## Quiz

1. What is quantum computing?
   - **A)** Classical computing
   - **B)** Computing using quantum mechanics with qubits
   - **C)** Normal computing
   - **D)** Slow computing

2. What is neuromorphic computing?
   - **A)** Traditional computing
   - **B)** Brain-inspired computing with neuromorphic chips
   - **C)** Quantum computing
   - **D)** Cloud computing

3. What is edge AI?
   - **A)** Cloud AI
   - **B)** AI processing at edge devices for low latency
   - **C)** Server AI
   - **D)** Centralized AI

**Answers:**
1. **B** - Quantum computing uses quantum mechanics (superposition, entanglement) with qubits for potentially exponential speedup
2. **B** - Neuromorphic computing mimics brain structure with spiking neural networks on specialized chips
3. **B** - Edge AI processes AI workloads locally on edge devices, reducing latency and improving privacy
