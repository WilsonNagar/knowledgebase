# High Availability Patterns

## Overview

High availability ensures systems remain operational. This guide covers redundancy, failover mechanisms, disaster recovery, multi-region deployments, and building resilient backend systems.

## Deep Explanation

### Availability Levels

**99%**: 87.6 hours downtime/year
**99.9%**: 8.76 hours downtime/year
**99.99%**: 52.56 minutes downtime/year

### Patterns

**Active-Passive**: Standby servers
**Active-Active**: All servers active
**Multi-Region**: Geographic distribution
